Who we are?

Julo Finance adalah applikasi web yang bersifat sebagai aggregator dan penyedia solusi lander and borrower (Pemberi dan Penerima Kredit).


Perusahaan ini didirikan dengan tujuan menyediakan platform keuangan yang mudah digunakan dimana dunia bisnis bisa melakukan bla2 yang kompetitif. Di dukung dengan pengetahuan dan keahlian veteran di industri ini.

In simplified terms Julo Finance Logistic is a web based finance business and hohoho (see the "How do we do it section" for more information).


JULO provides affordable unsecured personal loan (KTA) exclusively to smartphone users


More than 100M Indonesians are marginalized by formal lending institutions, exploited by loan sharks, or do not have valuable possessions to be collateralized or pawned. JULO extends affordable credit to this excluded segment by utilizing state-of-the-art technologies. The entire lending process is done via smartphone for user convenience, to maximize penetration nationwide, and to enable use of rich data for verification and risk assessment purposes.
